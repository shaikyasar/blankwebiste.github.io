POLLUTION AND POLLUTION CONTROL



Pollution is the introduction of contaminants into the natural environment that cause adverse change. Pollution can take the form of chemical substances or energy, such as noise ,heat or light.Pollutants, the components of pollution,canbe either foreign substances/energies or naturally occurring contaminants. Pollution is often classed as point source or nonpoint source pollution.

There are mainly four types of pollution shown:

  • air pollution
  • water pollution
  • soil pollution
  • noise pollution
